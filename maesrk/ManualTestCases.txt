Test Scenarios

against the email field.

displayed against the email field.
3. To verify all the required fields by not filling any data and directly clicking on login button.
4. To verify that the "remember me" check box is functioning as expected.
5. To verify that the alignment of all the fields is correct.

is displayed.
7. To verify that when both email and password combination is correct user is successfully logged in .
8. To verify that the password field is encrypted.
9. To verify that on click of login button a post api is being hit .
